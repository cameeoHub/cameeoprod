About

Hello and welcome to the Cameeo Productions website.
A message from creator Jacob Kendall...

"You may find yourself here as somebody who is quite familiar with our work, or maybe you have been sent our way by a happy customer or a google search...
Either way, it's no skin off our nose to tell you what we here at Cameeo Prod. are all about.
-
Cameeo Productions was created by myself only a year ago. The first idea behind the company, was to create an open space for the enjoyment of human expression. Beginning with the mediums of videography, spoken word and dance - I built a portfolio of expression and my online pressence as Cameeo. The initial motive behind the name being, to make an anonymous title to explore under. Coming from a very creative educational background, I found that it was very easy as a young creative in the days of social media, to surround yourself with an echo chamber. So I decided to begin my new creative process under a name I knew no-one close would recognise. I wanted to have an honest response. To see if my work got recognition on its very own merit - without the obviously much appreciated support of creative friends.
-
My idea in the beginning was to have a space where I could explore my own practices - but also where others could find common place with my words or ideas. I never wanted to be too opinionated. The goal was never to have people listen to what I was saying and agree. It was important that people levelled with what was being said on an emotional level.
I wanted to speak to the heart. I began creating movement and dance content. The reason I started here is because I already had a foundation in video editing and had always wanted to explore my passion for movement as a form of expression. I saw this as the perfect springboard to find my confidence in editing again and also renew my ideas about expression.
During this journey and building my brand - for the first time, I started to get to grips with how to brand my content. Starting with logo editing, moving all the way through to website development. I attended courses and now knew the direction I wanted to head in with my work and career ambitions.
-
Now, Cameeo Productions is a fully-fledged company dedicated to all my beginning wishes and more. We create expressive content through mediums such as; photography, videography, movement, spoken-word, audiography, music, dance, content-creation and many more.
We continue to provide an open space for creatives looking to improve their repertoire. And now, we even offer brand building, in a step-by-step package tailored to your needs. Including continued support regarding your content creation.
I am so proud of how far this project has come and am very lucky to have the fantastically talented team that I have with me today. Please find more about what we can do for you on our services page. We look forward to hearing from you and to helping develop your skills and content."
